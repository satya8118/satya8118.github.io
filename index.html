About

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Shiv Nadar Institution of Eminence, Dadri (Greater Noida), where I have been teaching since 2012. My academic journey spans over three decades, beginning with a Master's in Mathematics from Andhra University and culminating in a PhD from IIT Kanpur in 2012.

My doctoral thesis, titled Matrix Algebra and Graph Theory, was supervised by Professor A. K. Lal and Professor S. K. Mehta. Since then, I have mentored eight PhD students and published extensively across combinatorial matrix theory and algebraic number theory.

I am deeply committed to mathematics education and have been an active resource person for the Mathematics Training and Talent Search (MTTS) programme, funded by the National Board for Higher Mathematics (NBHM), across institutions throughout India.
